About Me

Welcome to my blog. My name is Garrett Stanifer and I am a student at IUPUI studying cybersecurity. I am a second year student at IUPUI, but a first year cybersecurity student. The majority of my study takes place in the classroom but I do try to learn some things outside of class. I do this with tools like TryHackMe and YouTube that I feel really help me master some basic knowledge.

Why CyberSecurity?

After having a subpar year in the Mechanical Engineering major, I decided to change to cybersecurity. I decided to pursue cybersecurity because I have always been good with computers and also really enjoy working with them. So far, I am having a much better time with this degree and feel like I am learning things I will actually use in my future career. My plan is to still graduate on time, even with changing majors. I will do this by either adding some extra classes each semester or by participating in summer classes offered. I will continue to update this blog as my education continues.
